# Press Kit

## Company
RefundWala is an India-first platform helping consumers recover stuck refunds and helping merchants resolve disputes with speed, accountability, and auditability.

## Mission
Make refund recovery as simple as submitting a ticket, while driving trust across the consumer ecosystem.

## Positioning
“We recover your stuck refunds fast — and you only pay when we win.”

## Product Snapshot
- Concierge-led dispute resolution
- Evidence vault with immutable hashing
- SLA-backed escalation workflows
- Enterprise-ready audit logging

## Demo Statistics (Seeded)
- 1,200 cases
- 200 consumers
- 100 merchants
- 25 agents
- 2,000+ evidence records
